Pineapple-Infused Vodka

Description

This infused vodka will keep for approximately 2 weeks in the fridge after it is strained. Serve shaken in a martini glass and enjoy!© By sd061507

Ingredients

Original recipe yields 17 servings

  • 1 fresh pineapple, peeled and sliced
  • 1 (750 milliliter) bottle vodka (such as Stolichnaya®)

Steps

  1. Stack pineapple slices in a circle around the edges of a large glass jar, leaving the center open. Pour vodka into jar, completely covering pineapple. Seal jar and store in a cool, dark place for 10 days.
  2. Remove pineapple and strain vodka through a mesh strainer back into a jar; seal and refrigerate.
